Big Bend National Park Hiking Trails

Big Bend National Park is beautiful wilderness area located in Southern Texas. The park offers over 150 miles to wander through. Hikers can explore the desert land or travel up the mountain trails to visit beautiful flora and fauna, geology, and unique natural features. For those seeking solitdue or an adventure into the wilderness there are many oppurtuniteis for backpacking, river trips, or primitive camping.
